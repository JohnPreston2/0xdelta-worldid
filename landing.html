// Core Forensic Metrics
FHS
Forensic Health Score
Composite score aggregating all signals. Weights liquidity, flow quality, holder distribution and technical momentum.
RANGE: 0.0 → 10.0 · ALERT BELOW 4.0
NBP
Net Buying Pressure
24h delta between buy and sell flows, normalized by volume. Detects accumulation vs distribution phases.
RANGE: -100% → +100%
ICR
Impact Crash Risk
Estimated price drop if top holder exits. Computed from liquidity depth vs top wallet size.
THRESHOLD: >1.5 CRITICAL
LCR
Liquidity Coverage Ratio
Pool liquidity relative to market cap. Low LCR signals shallow market, vulnerable to manipulation.
DANGER BELOW 30%
BPI
Breakout Potential Index
Multi-factor signal combining volume surge, wallet accumulation and RSI divergence.
SIGNAL ABOVE 0.65
WCC
Whale Concentration Coefficient
Gini-like measure of supply concentration across top 20 wallets. High WCC increases rug risk.
DANGER ABOVE 40%
